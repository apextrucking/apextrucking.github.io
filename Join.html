Interested?

Joining is quick and simple. We always welcome new Drivers, and we can't wait to have you as one of us!

Requirements

To ensure the integrity of our VTC and to continue keeping the environment fun to be in, there are some basic requirements:

  • Aged 14+
  • 50+ Hours in ATS, ETS2 or combined
  • Good understanding of English
  • Able to follow TruckersMP Rules AT ALL TIMES
  • Have tag ALWAYS set to the VTC's tag (ATVTC)

How to join ATVTC

If you are interested in joining ATVTC, just join our Discord Server

From there, the very helpful staff (including our very own Recruitment Manager) can guide you through joining us!

We can't wait to get you on the road!
